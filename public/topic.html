December 4, 2023
IN
FASHION

Christian Dior launches in 1947 the New Look, a figure that emphasizes large skirts, close-fitting bodies and small waists. After years of war and economic deprivation, Dior brought back the desire for glamour and femininity with his "flower woman" design.
