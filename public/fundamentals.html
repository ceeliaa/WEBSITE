FUNDAMENTALS OF COMPUTER ENGINEERING


During the first semester we have learned with this subject not only the history of computer engineering, but also basic aspects of computer hardware and software and we have finally received an introduction to software development, allowing us to create our first web page.


Nowadays technology is advancing rapidly, so it is very important to have the knowledge to understand what is happening and how to respond to it. This course has given us the basis that we will need from now on to become computer engineers in the future.
